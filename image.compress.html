How Our Image Compressor Works

Our image compression tool uses modern browser APIs to process your images locally without uploading them to any server. Here's how it works:

  1. Select your image - Drag and drop or click to browse files on your device.
  2. Adjust settings - Choose your preferred compression level and output format.
  3. Compress - Click the compress button to optimize your image.
  4. Download - Save the compressed image to your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, and WEBP formats. You can compress these formats or convert between them.

Is there a file size limit?

The current limit is 10MB per image. This ensures fast processing in your browser.

Are my images stored anywhere?

No, all processing happens in your browser. We don't upload or store your images on any server.

What's the best format for web use?

WEBP generally offers the best compression with good quality. JPEG is good for photos, while PNG is better for graphics with transparency.
